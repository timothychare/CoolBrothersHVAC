About Cool Brothers HVAC

Over 25 years of professional HVAC experience serving residential and commercial customers. Licensed, insured, and committed to your comfort.

Our Story

Founded by two brothers with a passion for keeping people comfortable

Family-Owned Excellence Since 1998

Cool Brothers HVAC was founded when brothers Mike and Dave Rodriguez realized their community needed reliable, honest HVAC service. What started as two brothers in a pickup truck has grown into a trusted team of certified technicians serving thousands of satisfied customers.

We built our business on simple principles: treat every customer like family, provide upfront honest pricing, and never compromise on quality. These values continue to guide us today as we've grown into one of the region's most trusted HVAC companies.

From emergency repairs at 2 AM to complex commercial installations, we bring the same level of professionalism and care to every job. Because at Cool Brothers, your comfort is our mission.
